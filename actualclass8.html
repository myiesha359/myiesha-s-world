AI/ML engineering is the field of designing, developing, and deploying artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) systems. AI/ML engineers work on a variety of tasks, including:
Analyzing data AI/ML engineers analyze large amounts of data to determine which machine learning algorithms are best for a given problem
Designing models AI/ML engineers design, train, and optimize machine learning models
Collaborating with others AI/ML engineers work with other teams, such as data scientists, software engineers, and product managers, to integrate AI/ML capabilities into products and services
Ensuring security AI/ML engineers ensure that their solutions are secure, scalable, and efficient
Improving performance AI/ML engineers continuously evaluate and improve the performance of their models
